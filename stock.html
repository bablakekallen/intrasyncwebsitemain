Built for Precast Component Storage

Yard management challenges unique to large, heavy precast components require specialized solutions.

Component Aging Tracking: Production date on every piece, identify aged inventory for priority shipping, FIFO enforcement, and warranty expiration warnings.
Weight & Dimension Management: Accurate component weights for crane planning, dimensional data for truck loading optimization, and yard space calculations.
Customer Segregation: Components tagged by project and customer, prevent cross-project shipment errors, and customer-specific quality holds management.
Weather Protection Management: Track covered vs. exposed storage locations, tarping requirements by component type, and freeze-thaw cycle monitoring.
Load Staging Optimization: Pre-stage components for upcoming deliveries, minimize crane moves and handling time, and optimize truck loading sequence.
