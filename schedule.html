Built for Precast Manufacturing

Production scheduling challenges unique to precast concrete require specialized solutions.

Cure Time Management: Automated scheduling respects concrete cure times, optimizes steam cure vs. ambient cure, and adjusts for weather impact on cure schedules.
Mold & Bed Optimization: Maximize bed utilization, minimize mold changeovers, prioritize complex shapes, and optimize multi-cavity bed planning.
Delivery Date Commitment: Customer promise dates drive schedule priorities, transportation time included in planning, and crane availability at jobsite considered.
Multi-Project Balancing: Optimize across all active projects, prevent single-project bottlenecks, and balance workload for steady crew utilization.
Seasonal Demand Planning: Construction season forecasting, winter capacity planning, holiday shutdown scheduling, and weather delay buffers.
