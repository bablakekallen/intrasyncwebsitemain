Detailed Comparisons

CastLogic vs BETSY Legacy

BETSY has been around for decades, but as a legacy solution it receives infrequent updates and lacks modern capabilities. It focuses primarily on scheduling and production tracking, requiring additional software for accounting, HR, and other business functions.

Key Differences:

  • + CastLogic: Actively developed with regular feature updates
  • + CastLogic: Complete ERP with integrated accounting, HR, and payroll
  • + CastLogic: AI-powered scheduling optimization
  • + CastLogic: Modern cloud-native architecture with mobile apps
  • + CastLogic: CAD/BIM integration (Revit, AutoCAD) and barcode/RFID scanning

Best for: Manufacturers who want a modern, actively-maintained platform instead of legacy software

CastLogic vs Titan3000 Legacy

Titan3000 is another legacy solution that receives infrequent updates. Like BETSY, it's focused on the production side and requires integration with external accounting systems. It lacks modern capabilities like CAD/BIM integration and barcode scanning.

Key Differences:

  • + CastLogic: Actively developed with regular feature updates
  • + CastLogic: Real-time job costing with WIP accounting
  • + CastLogic: CAD/BIM integration (Revit, AutoCAD) and barcode/RFID scanning
  • + CastLogic: Perpetual inventory synced with COGS
  • + CastLogic: Flexible payroll with daily pay option

Best for: Companies ready to move from outdated legacy systems to modern technology

CastLogic vs IDAT-ERP

IDAT-ERP is a comprehensive European solution with strong capabilities. However, its European heritage can mean different compliance requirements and support availability for North American operations.

Key Differences:

  • + CastLogic: Built for US compliance (Davis-Bacon, prevailing wage)
  • + CastLogic: North America-based support team
  • + CastLogic: AI/ML optimization throughout
  • + CastLogic: NPCA and PCI member integration

Best for: US and Canadian precast manufacturers needing local compliance and support

CastLogic vs Spreadsheets + QuickBooks

Many precast manufacturers still rely on Excel for scheduling, QuickBooks for accounting, and various other tools. This creates data silos, manual reconciliation nightmares, and no real-time visibility.

Key Differences:

  • + CastLogic: Zero manual data entry between systems
  • + CastLogic: Close books in hours, not days
  • + CastLogic: Real-time profitability by project
  • + CastLogic: Eliminate version control issues

Best for: Companies ready to eliminate manual processes and gain visibility
