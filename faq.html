Everything you need to know about CastLogic ERP for precast manufacturing

General Questions

What is CastLogic?

CastLogic is a complete ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) system built specifically for precast concrete manufacturing. Unlike generic ERP systems, CastLogic is designed from the ground up to handle the unique challenges of precast production including:

  • Complex production scheduling with mold constraints
  • Concrete curing time management
  • Multi-stage quality control and testing
  • GPS-based yard inventory tracking
  • Job costing with WIP accounting
  • Integration with BIM/CAD design files

The system integrates all aspects of your operation into one unified platform, eliminating data silos and manual processes.

Who is CastLogic designed for?

CastLogic is designed for precast concrete manufacturers of all sizes, from small family-owned operations to large multi-plant enterprises. We serve every segment of the precast industry:

  • Architectural Precast: Cladding panels, facades, GFRC, custom finishes, complex embeds
  • Structural & Prestressed: Beams, columns, hollow-core, double-tees, parking structures, stadium risers
  • Utility Products: Manholes, catch basins, vaults, boxes, electrical enclosures, septic tanks
  • Concrete Pipe: Storm pipe, sanitary sewer, culvert, RCP, RCCP, box culverts

No matter what you manufacture, CastLogic adapts to your specific molds, curing processes, quality requirements, and production workflows. Whether you're running one plant or ten, CastLogic scales to meet your needs.

What makes CastLogic different from other ERP systems?

CastLogic is purpose-built for precast manufacturing, not a generic system adapted for the industry. Key differentiators include:

  • Industry Expertise: Built by team with 50+ years combined precast experience
  • AI Scheduling: Machine learning optimizes production schedules automatically
  • Pre-configured: 80% setup out-of-box vs. months of customization
  • Mobile-First: Full functionality on tablets and phones for plant floor
  • Visual Controls: GPS yard maps, Gantt charts, real-time dashboards
  • Support: Team that understands precast operations, not just software

Is IntraSync Industrial certified or accredited?

Yes, IntraSync Industrial is a proud member of both:

  • NPCA (National Precast Concrete Association): Ensuring our software aligns with industry best practices and manufacturing standards
  • PCI (Precast/Prestressed Concrete Institute): Staying current with quality standards, engineering practices, and industry innovations

These memberships demonstrate our commitment to the precast industry and ensure CastLogic is built with authentic industry knowledge, not generic ERP adapted for precast.

Features & Functionality

Does CastLogic integrate with BIM and CAD software?

Yes, CastLogic seamlessly integrates with major BIM and CAD platforms including:

  • Autodesk Revit
  • AutoCAD
  • Tekla Structures
  • Other IFC-compatible design tools

This integration allows for automatic transfer of design specifications, piece marks, quantities, and production requirements directly from design files into your production schedule. Changes in design files can trigger automatic updates to schedules and material requirements.

Can CastLogic handle multiple plant locations?

Yes, CastLogic is designed for multi-plant operations. You can:

  • Manage production and resources across all facilities from a single dashboard
  • Balance workload between plants automatically
  • Transfer jobs between facilities
  • Maintain separate accounting and reporting for each location
  • Share inventory and resources across locations
  • Consolidate reporting at corporate level

Each plant can operate independently while corporate has complete visibility and control.

How does the AI scheduling work?

CastLogic uses machine learning to create optimal production schedules. The AI:

  • Learns from your historical production data and patterns
  • Considers mold availability, curing times, and capacity constraints
  • Factors in material availability and delivery requirements
  • Optimizes crew assignments and workload balance
  • Continuously improves based on actual vs. planned performance
  • Automatically adjusts when priorities or requirements change

Customers typically see up to 10% reduction in lead times and improved on-time delivery after implementation.

Can CastLogic track material costs in real-time?

Yes, CastLogic provides comprehensive real-time cost tracking:

  • Automatic inventory valuation updates as materials are consumed
  • Real-time COGS (Cost of Goods Sold) calculation
  • Tracking of price fluctuations from suppliers
  • Job-level cost tracking with labor, materials, and overhead allocation
  • WIP (Work in Progress) accounting
  • Variance analysis between estimated and actual costs

Financial reports update in real-time as production progresses, giving you accurate profitability insights at any moment.

Does CastLogic support certified payroll and prevailing wage?

Yes, CastLogic includes full certified payroll functionality for Davis-Bacon and prevailing wage compliance:

  • Automatic wage rate determination by classification and location
  • WH-347 form generation
  • Fringe benefit tracking and reporting
  • Complete audit trails
  • Integration with time tracking to capture hours by project
  • Electronic submission capabilities

The system ensures compliance while minimizing administrative burden.

Does CastLogic include design tools or do I need separate CAD software?

It depends on what you manufacture:

Utility & Pipe Producers - Integrated Design Tool Included:

  • Design manholes, septic tanks, catch basins, vaults, pipes, and box culverts directly in CastLogic
  • No separate CAD software needed for standard utility products
  • Designs flow instantly into estimates, quotes, and production
  • 2D preview, BOMs, and production drawings auto-generated

Architectural & Structural - CAD Integration:

  • Seamlessly integrates with Autodesk Revit, AutoCAD, Tekla Structures
  • Import design specs, quantities, and piece marks automatically
  • No manual re-entry of engineering data

This gives utility/pipe producers a significant cost advantage—no AutoCAD or Revit licenses needed for everyday production.

Technical Questions

What are the system requirements?

CastLogic is a web-based application accessible via modern browsers:

  • Desktop: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge (latest versions)
  • Tablets: iPad, Android tablets, Windows tablets
  • Phones: iOS 14+, Android 10+
  • Internet: Stable broadband connection (5 Mbps minimum)
  • Hardware: No special hardware required, works on existing computers

For on-premise installations, we provide detailed server specifications based on your company size.

How secure is my data?

Industry-standard security measures protect your data:

  • TLS 1.2+ encryption for all data in transit
  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • Multi-factor authentication (MFA) available
  • Role-based access controls
  • Automated daily backups with 30-day retention
  • Complete audit trails of all system activity

Your data is isolated in a secure tenant and never shared across customers.

Can I export my data if I need to?

Yes, you own your data and can export it anytime:

  • Export to Excel, CSV, or PDF from any report
  • API access for programmatic data extraction
  • Complete database export available on request
  • No data retention or export fees

If you ever decide to leave CastLogic, we provide full assistance with data export in your format of choice.
