PCI Plant Certification Support

The Precast/Prestressed Concrete Institute (PCI) plant certification program ensures consistent quality through rigorous standards. CastLogic provides the documentation and traceability tools you need to maintain certification with confidence.

Learn more about PCI certification at pci.org

How CastLogic Supports PCI Certification

  • PCI-Aligned Inspection Checklists

    Digital checklists configured for PCI requirements with mandatory fields and approval workflows

  • Automated Audit Documentation

    One-click generation of audit packages with quality records, test results, and traceability documentation

  • Certification Status Tracking

    Monitor certification expiration dates and renewal requirements across product categories

  • NCR & Corrective Action Workflows

    Structured non-conformance reporting with root cause analysis and corrective action tracking

  • Strength Test Integration

    Track 7, 14, and 28-day cylinder tests with automated alerts for release strength requirements

  • Prestress Documentation

    Digital capture of strand tensioning, elongation measurements, and release records with engineer approvals

How CastLogic Supports EPD Generation

  • Mix Design Tracking

    Every batch linked to specific mix designs with complete material proportions for carbon footprint calculations

  • Supplier Certification Management

    Track mill certificates, material test reports, and environmental data from cement, aggregate, and admixture suppliers

  • Production Energy Tracking

    Capture energy consumption per production run for accurate Life Cycle Assessment (LCA) data

  • Carbon Footprint Data

    Calculate embodied carbon per component based on actual material usage, not industry averages

  • LCA Data Export

    Export production data in formats ready for third-party EPD verification programs

  • LEED & Buy Clean Documentation

    Generate documentation packages for LEED certification and Buy Clean procurement compliance

Environmental Product Declaration (EPD) Support

Environmental Product Declarations provide transparent, verified information about a product's environmental impact throughout its lifecycle. With growing demand for sustainable construction, EPDs have become essential for winning projects with LEED requirements or Buy Clean procurement policies.

CastLogic captures the production data you need to generate accurate, product-specific EPDs rather than relying on industry-average data.
